Hi! My name is Daniel

Hi! I'm Daniel Lombraña González, a Computer Engineer with a PhD that loves open source software and new technologies. Currently I'm working for the Citizen Cyberscience Centre as a researcher and a developer. I also collaborate with the Open Knowledge Foundation. Check the projects in which I have been involved!
