Photography

One of my hobbies is photography. I take pictures of almost everything, nature, people, places, etc.

As an amateur one of my biggests successes with photography was to get one of my photos selected to be part of the first Ubuntu OS contest for wallpapers and included in the default wallpaper package of the distribution, that will be downloaded by hundreds of people.

All my photos are released with a Creative Commons license (Attribution-ShareAlike 2.0 Generic), so feel free to use them!
