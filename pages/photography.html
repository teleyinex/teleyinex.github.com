Photography

I love photography. In my free time, I take pictures of almost everything, nature, people, places, etc.

As an amateur one of my biggests successes has been to win a place in the first Ubuntu OS contest for desktop wallpapers. The photo was included in the default CD-ROM and distributed as part of the wallpaper package, sharing my work with hundreds of people.

More recently, the Spanish company Lanjarón used another photo from my portfolio to promote their water.

As an open source believer, all my photos are released with a Creative Commons license (Attribution-ShareAlike 2.0 Generic), so feel free to use them!
